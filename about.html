ABOUT
Ellis Green is a jazz bass player from Phoenix, Arizona. He began playing the upright bass in college, where he became interested in jazz. Since then, he has also occasionally performed on the piano. During his time participating in the Jazz Studies program at Arizona State University, Ellis had the opportunity to study with teachers including Michael Kocour and Lewis Nash.

Ellis has played at many venues around the Valley including The Nash, the premier jazz club in the Phoenix area. In the summer of 2018, he headlined a show at The Nash as part of its "Emerging Artist" series, performing a program of piano trio interpretations of compositions by Tadd Dameron. Those trio interpretations were later adapted and recorded for Tadd!, his first album.
