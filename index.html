Local, personal, and professional
Ellis Green Music provides elegant, engaging live jazz music, whether in a concert setting or as background music for weddings, restaurants, hotels, and parties. Ellis Green is a jazz bassist/pianist and a Phoenix native who has performed at prestigious local venues including LON's at the Hermosa Inn, the Biltmore, the Phoenician, and Wrigley Mansion, for audiences including Tempe mayor Corey Woods and Arizona governor Katie Hobbs. Ellis isn't some out-of-state booking agent- he'll work with you personally to bring great music for the occasion. Ellis Green Music is a hassle-free option for live music that is already a proven hit with audiences around the Valley.
