Local, personal, and professional
Ellis Green Music provides elegant, engaging live jazz music, whether in a concert setting or as background music for weddings, restaurants, hotels, and parties.

Ellis Green is a jazz pianist/bassist and a Phoenix native. For nearly ten years Ellis has been performing around the valley at prestigious local venues including LON's at the Hermosa Inn, the Phoenician, and Scottsdale Fashion Square. See more below.

During his career Ellis has performed at events for clients ranging from ASU to the NFL, and for audiences including the mayors of Phoenix and Tempe and the governor of Arizona.

Ellis regularly performs in different configurations, whether as a solo pianist or as a trio with upright bass and drums— whichever is best for a client's budget/space. See some examples on the Videos page.
