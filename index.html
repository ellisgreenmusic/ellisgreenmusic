Ellis Green is a jazz bass player from Phoenix, Arizona. During his time with the Jazz Studies program at Arizona State University, he had the opportunity to study with pianist Michael Kocour.

Ellis has played at several venues around the Valley, including The Nash, the premier jazz club in the Phoenix area. He has performed with artists including Bruce Forman, Michael Kocour, and Lewis Nash.

Tadd!
June 2019

Listen now on Bandcamp.

Tadd! features trio interpretations of music written by composer and pianist Tadd Dameron. It was recorded in the fall of 2018 by Clarke Rigsby of Tempest Recording.

The album features Ellis Green on piano, Max Beckman on bass, and Filip Tipei on drums.

Track list (all tracks composed by Tadd Dameron):
1.The Scene is Clean
2.Dameronia
3.If You Could See Me Now
4.Casbah
5.Dial 'B' for Beauty
6.Our Delight
